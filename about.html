Who We Are

We are the Environment Club of Burnside High School. Run by the beloved teacher, Jon Land, we fight to keep our land healthy and clean for our school.
It can be a difficult task, but we achieve our goal by cleaning litter, introducing more native plants, mulching and watering our existing bush.
